Blended to Perfection Self-advocacy

Key Areas of Focus

  • Choice-making: about activities and within activities.
  • Assertiveness: appropriate ways to refuse or state wants/needs.
  • Appropriate Relationships Interaction: Building and maintaining friendships.
  • Opportunities for day-to-day planning activities: Involvement in organizing daily tasks and responsibilities.
  • Making informed choices: discussion of consequences (both positive and negative) regarding choices.
  • Personal awareness and safety: other assistance in increasing control over the individuals' own life/environment.

Delicious Treats, Good Eats Pre-vocational

Vocational Training

Individuals are encouraged to express their given preference for individual vocational pursuits. ADE provides pre-vocational training and support to assist individuals in developing job skills and work habits for possible future paid employment. Focus areas include listening skills and following directions for an individual to be successful in an integrated work setting.

  • Janitorial
  • Organization Skills
  • Recycling
  • Messenger
  • Personalize to meet the interests of individuals.
